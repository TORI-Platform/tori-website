Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters at TORÍ! At TORÍ, we value your trust. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. By using TORÍ, you agree to this policy.

Information We Collect

  • 1. Personal Details: When you sign up, we may collect your name, email, and payment details (if you purchase stories).
  • 2. Usage Data: We collect information about how you use TORÍ, such as the stories you read and device type.
  • 3. Writer Information: If you join TORÍ as a writer, we may collect additional information related to your author profile.

Children’s Privacy

TORÍ is not designed for children under 13. If we find that we have collected information from a child under this age, we will delete it immediately.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  1. Provide you with access to African stories and authors.
  2. Improve your recommendations and reading experience.
  3. Process payments securely.
  4. Send you updates about new features, stories, and events.

Sharing Your Information

TORÍ does not sell your personal data. We may share limited information with:

  1. Payment providers to process transactions.
  2. Analytics providers to help us improve TORÍ.
  3. Authorities if required by law.

Data Security

We take steps to protect your data from unauthorized access or misuse. However, no online system is 100% secure, so please keep your account details safe.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  1. Update or correct your account details.
  2. Request deletion of your account.
  3. Opt out of marketing communications.

Update to Terms

TORÍ may update these Terms and Conditions occasionally. We will notify you of any major changes. Continued use of the platform means you accept the updated terms.

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If major changes are made, we will notify you.

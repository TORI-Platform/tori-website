FAQs

General questions

🔎
When is TORÍ set to launch?
We're working hard to bring TORÍ to you soon. Join the waitlist to be the first to know when we launch.
Can I download the app now?
Not yet. The app will be available on launch. In the meantime, stay updated by subscribing to our newsletter.
Can I read on the website?
Yes. TORÍ offers a web experience so you can enjoy stories on any device without installing the app.
Do I have to pay to access TORÍ?
TORÍ will include free and premium experiences. Pricing and plans will be announced closer to launch.
Which countries will TORÍ be available in?
We aim to launch in multiple regions, starting with Africa and expanding globally. Availability will be announced at launch.
How do I join the waitlist?
Click “Become a writer” in the navigation or use the waitlist form in the popup to subscribe.
What kinds of stories can I read?
From contemporary fiction to folklore, poetry, essays, and more—curated to celebrate African creativity.
Will there be offline reading?
Offline reading is on our roadmap for the mobile app. We'll share more details post-launch.
Can I follow my favorite writers?
Yes. You'll be able to follow creators and get notified when they publish new work.
Is there a way to save stories?
You'll be able to bookmark stories to revisit them anytime.
How do I publish my work on TORÍ?
Join the waitlist as a writer. We'll invite early creators with instructions on how to publish.
Do writers get paid?
We're exploring creator-friendly monetization. We'll announce revenue options as we roll out features.
What formats can I publish?
Stories, poetry, essays, and serialized content. More formats will be added over time.
Can I import my work from other platforms?
We'll provide simple tools and guides to help you bring your work to TORÍ.
